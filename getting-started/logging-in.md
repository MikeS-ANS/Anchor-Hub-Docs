# Logging In

Anchor Hub uses your existing Anchor Network Solutions Microsoft account — no separate username or password to remember.

## First Launch

1. Open **Anchor Hub** from the Start Menu or desktop shortcut
2. Click **Sign in with Microsoft**
3. A browser window opens — sign in with your `@anchornetworksolutions.com` account
4. Once signed in, the browser window closes and the app loads automatically

Your session stays active. You won't be prompted to sign in again unless your token expires (typically after several weeks of inactivity).

## Switching Accounts

If the wrong account is signed in:

1. Click your **profile photo** in the top-right corner of the app
2. Click **Sign Out**
3. Sign in again with the correct account

## Troubleshooting Login

**"You don't have access to this application"**
Your account hasn't been granted an Anchor Hub role yet. Contact Mike to get access assigned in Azure.

**Browser window opens but nothing happens after signing in**
Close the browser window and try again. If it continues, restart the app.

**App shows a blank screen after login**
Try signing out and back in. If the issue persists, check your internet connection — the app requires connectivity to Azure for authentication.
